CAVA · DATA PLATFORM · 01 / 01
Architecture / How every source lands in one dashboard

Nine sources, one warehouse, one view of the business.

Every channel CAVA sells or markets on pushes into a single warehouse on a fixed cadence. Transformations turn raw feeds into four governed marts, and any BI front-end — the custom platform, Power BI, or Amazon QuickSight — reads the same numbers.

Cadence
Real-time to daily
Orders stream in live; ads and analytics land hourly; marketplace files and competitor scans land daily before 7 AM IST.
Quality
Tested at the door
dbt tests on every model — nulls, duplicates, reconciliation against settlement totals — block bad data before dashboards see it.
Governance
One metric dictionary
NSR, DOH, OSA and ROAS are defined once in the metrics layer; Power BI, QuickSight and the custom app cannot disagree.
Cost
Scale as needed
Start on Postgres + cron for near-zero cost; the same dbt models move to BigQuery/Snowflake unchanged when volume grows.
